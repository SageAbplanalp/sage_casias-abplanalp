Cat Care Basics

Here are some essential tips for keeping your cat healthy and happy:

  • Provide fresh water daily.
  • Feed high-quality cat food.
  • Brush regularly depending on breed.
  • Schedule annual vet checkups.
  • Give plenty of playtime and enrichment.
