Safety & contact

Electricity is useful, but it must always be treated with care and respect.

Electrical safety basics

  • Never touch damaged power lines.
  • Keep electrical devices away from water.
  • Do not overload outlets or extension cords.
  • Use trained professionals for major electrical work.

Safety education is an important part of understanding the grid. People should know how to avoid hazards and respond responsibly.
